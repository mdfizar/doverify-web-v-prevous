Anti-Counterfeit Processes

How doverify product Authentication will work

QR Code Generation

Each product is assigned a unique QR code that is generated during the production process. This code contains information specific to that particular product, such as a serial number, batch number, or other identifiers

Incorporation into Packaging

The unique QR code is printed or embedded onto the product packaging, label, or the product itself. This ensures that each product has its own distinct code.
